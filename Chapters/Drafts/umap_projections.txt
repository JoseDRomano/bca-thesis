\subsubsection{UMAP Projection}
Figure~\ref{fig:kmeans_umap} shows the UMAP \cite{mcinnes2018umap} projection of both cluster
assignments and ground-truth labels onto the same two-dimensional embedding. UMAP is used here
purely as a visualisation tool and it was not involved in the clustering itself. The juxtaposition
of the two panels allows visual assessment of the degree to which the K-Means partition
corresponds to the known subtype structure. A clear separation in the right panel (ground truth)
that aligns with a separation in the left panel (K-Means) provides geometric confirmation of
the purity values reported in Table~\ref{tab:kmeans_cluster_composition}.
